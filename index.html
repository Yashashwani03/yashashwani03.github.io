About Me

I am Yashashwani, a masters student with a deep interest in Condensed Matter Physics, especially the field of correlated electron systems and the emergence of novel phenomenon as a consequence. My interest in the field started upon my introduction to the idea of Superconductivity in my undergraduate years. The idea of electron-electron interactions leading to the emergence of a macroscopic quantum mechanical state got me invested in trying to understand what happens when electronic correlations dominate in a system and their consequences.
